Meet Our Esteemed Faculty at St. Mary's College of Pharmacy

At St. Mary's, our strength lies in our dedicated and knowledgeable faculty, comprising 22 accomplished members who are passionate about education and the pharmaceutical field.

Our faculty members bring a wealth of experience and expertise, nurturing an environment that fosters academic excellence, research, and professional growth among our students.

Total number of teaching staff- 22

Teaching Approach: Each member of our faculty brings a unique blend of academic rigor and practical experience to the classroom. They are committed to providing a stimulating learning environment, encouraging critical thinking, and guiding students in their academic pursuits.

Industry Experience: Many of our faculty members bring invaluable industry experience, having worked in various sectors of the pharmaceutical field. This real-world exposure enriches the learning process by bridging academic theory with practical application, offering insights into current industry trends and practices.

Research Pioneers: We take pride in our faculty's contributions to research and innovation within the pharmaceutical domain. Their ongoing research projects, publications, and collaborations with industry and academia contribute significantly to the advancement of pharmaceutical sciences.

Mentorship and Guidance: Beyond the classroom, our faculty members serve as mentors, guiding students in their academic endeavors and career aspirations. They are committed to nurturing the holistic development of our students, preparing them to excel in their chosen paths.

Commitment to Excellence: Our faculty members are committed to upholding the highest standards of education and ethics. Their dedication to academic excellence and unwavering support for our students define the cornerstone of our institution.
